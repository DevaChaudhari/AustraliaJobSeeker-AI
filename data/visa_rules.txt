Student Visa 500

- Can work 48 hours per fortnight during study periods
- Can work unlimited hours during course breaks
- Many employers still prefer PR or Citizens
- Jobs stating "Australian Citizen Only" are not eligible
- Jobs stating "Permanent Resident Required" are not eligible
- Jobs stating "Must have full working rights" may not be eligible

Temporary Graduate Visa 485

- Full work rights
- Can apply for most professional jobs

Subclass 482 Sponsorship

- Employer sponsorship required
- Job must support sponsorship

Permanent Resident / Citizen

- Eligible for all jobs
